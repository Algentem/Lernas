About me

First of all, I would like to thank you for using this site! I love Ido, and I hope you do to! My name is William Johnsson, I grew up in a multi cultured family, with parents and grandparents from vastly different locations. Sadly, growing up, I only learned Swedish (where I was born)! I would love to have learned Hungarian, but I wasn't thought, and Finnish isn't much easier, etc. Around 2013-14 I discovered International Auxiliary languages, and fell in love. I have been doing some amateur conlanging before, but I decided to start again, and now try to construct Auxiliary languages. I knew from when I first desovered Auxiliary languages that Esperanto had a big community, and was told it was "good", but from my past experience in conlanging, I couldn't stand some parts of Esperanto. Some time went, and I discovered Ido! At first I was shy, shy it would be "too much" like Esperanto, and because of that I didn't give it a shot. A few months had gone by, maybe a year (in the beginning of 2015), and I finaly was ready to take a shot at Ido, and I'm not lying here; I fell in love with it at first glance... and I couldn't stop learning it (immediately did all the courses I could, and read all of the websites i found!)
