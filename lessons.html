Welcome to the lessons selector!

Here you will see all of the courses in Ido! This course will teach you the fundamentals (la fundamentali) in Ido! Ido is a really easy language to learn, so it wont take long!

We will go through the alphabet (l'alfabeto), the pronunciation (la pronuncadi), the numbers (la numbri) and a few sentence examples (frazi exempli)!
