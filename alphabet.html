Hello and welcome to HODIE.io!

hodie /'ho.die/ adverbo - today


A free site where you can learn and strengthen your activity in the international auxiliary language Ido, an Esperanto reform.

The simple and strickt alphabet (and it's pronounciation):

If you know English, or at least are familiar with the English alphabet, you will learn the Ido alphabet without problem. If you arn't familiar with the English alphabet; worry not!, Idos alphabet is simple and strickt (and you'll have it easier learning English after!) Here is the alphabet (the letters with "/" after means that it is pronounced almost like English, and "-" means that dosn't reflect English; see below!):

A b C CH D E F G H I J K L M N O P Q QU R S SH T U V W X Y Z
