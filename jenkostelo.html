La Jenko-stelo

La Jenko-stelo (The Jenko Star); is one of the, but most common, symbol of Ido. It represents the La sis fonto lingui (The six front languages) that Ido was born from!

La Janko-stelo was designed to not be an uneven "star", so that it was unique! A big problem with symbols for Auxiliary languages, and other related things, is that they all choose a (too) simple symbol, for example a star. That in turn gets mixed up with other, exactly the same, stars, and one can not tell them apart!

La sis fonto lingui

La sis fonto lingui was choose because of their amount of speakers, and relatively shared vocabulary. Even though English is a Germanic language, and Russian is a Slavic language, do they share a lot of features! In fact, a few thousand years ago, they where both the same language, spoken in northen Asia! That is why for example the English word "young" is in Russian "юный" (approximate pronunciation with English: yooneej), and this is why for example Chineese was not choose as a front language, where "年輕" (nianquing) means "young", if that would be choose instead of "yuna", it would be less recognizable for more people that would understand it!
